CONCEPT & Idea
    The app is basically made out of the requirment of not being able to find readable and learnable
    guitar arrangements for music easily.

    The issue is basically that transcribing and arranging by ear is tough, hence a severe lack of sheet or
    tablatures and also of youtube or guided learning videos or playthroughs.

    Issues are augmented by polyphonic and single instrument arrangements being tough to make. Also the state
    of the art tech in the space of MIR(Music Information Retrieval) and Music Transcription(MT) use a lot of
    heuristic algorightms and are largely unsolved problems even with a lot of engineering done for specific use
    cases the results are less than 90% accurate for polyphonic,multi instrument music.
    Even the stem separation is not very clean although pretty damn good currently.

SCOPE
    This discussion is not for the backend and apis, but rather the studio ui-ux primarily and some appendages that
    are directly dependent on this.
    So I currently get a youtube music url from the user and download the audio in m4a format. Get the metadata,
    and also run and get lyrics from youtube and synced lyrics from the web if available. This is solved with ux
    being a library page with a search(also doubles as download url input if music is not available in library)
    and filters.
    The studio scaffold with transcription ui is also made as a prop for now and I have a working old demo
    currently.
